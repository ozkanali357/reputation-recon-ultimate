# Scoring Rubric for WithSecure Assessor

## Overview
This document outlines the scoring rubric used to evaluate the effectiveness and quality of the WithSecure Assessor. Each criterion is weighted based on its importance to the overall assessment process.

## Criteria

1. **Evidence & Citation Quality (24%)**
   - Strict adherence to a claim-evidence schema.
   - Inclusion of verbatim excerpts, URLs, timestamps, and cached snapshots.
   - Comparison of vendor-stated claims versus independent labels.

2. **Entity Resolution & Categorization (20%)**
   - Robust entity resolver that maps names, URLs, and SHA1 hashes to canonical vendor/product identifiers.
   - Effective taxonomy mapping and handling of ambiguities.
   - Unicode-safe processing.

3. **Technical Execution & Resilience (15%)**
   - Implementation of snapshot mode and local caching.
   - Robust handling of retries, rate limits, and timeouts.
   - Availability of an offline demo pack and comprehensive unit tests.

4. **Problem Fit & Clarity (15%)**
   - Creation of a CISO-ready one-pager that mirrors the prompt structure.
   - Clear pathways for addressing "Insufficient public evidence" scenarios.

5. **Security Posture Synthesis (12%)**
   - Concise and structured summary of security posture with rationale.

6. **Trust/Risk Score Transparency (8%)**
   - Publication of the scoring formula.
   - Provision of per-signal scores and confidence levels.

7. **Alternatives & Quick Compare (6%)**
   - Side-by-side comparison of products.
   - Suggestions for 1-2 safer alternatives.

## Scoring Methodology
Each criterion will be evaluated based on the following scale:
- 0: Not met
- 1: Partially met
- 2: Fully met

The total score will be calculated by summing the weighted scores across all criteria. The maximum possible score is 100.

## Conclusion
This rubric serves as a guideline for assessing the quality and effectiveness of the WithSecure Assessor. Adherence to these criteria will ensure a comprehensive and reliable evaluation process.
